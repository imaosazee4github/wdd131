Benin City, Nigeria

Nigeria is a country located in West Africa and is the most populous nation on the continent, with over 200 million people. Its capital city is Abuja, while Lagos is the largest city and a major economic hub. Nigeria is known for its diverse cultures, languages, and ethnic groups—over 500 languages are spoken, with English as the official language. The country is rich in natural resources, especially oil and gas, and has a vibrant economy. Nigeria is also famous for its contributions to music, film (Nollywood), fashion, literature, and sports, making it one of Africa’s cultural powerhouses.
